The same teaching arc Raven uses to onboard collaborators to the VQE/LIGO project, packaged as a 12-module course.

**Modules**

1. Linear algebra refresher (the parts that matter for QM)
2. Qubits, single-qubit gates, the Bloch sphere
3. Multi-qubit systems, entanglement, the canonical states
4. PennyLane and Qiskit — same problem, two libraries
5. Variational circuits and parameter shift rules
6. Encoding classical data: amplitude, angle, basis
7. Loss landscapes and barren plateaus
8. Running on real hardware: noise, decoherence, mitigation
9. Quantum kernel methods
10. The VQE for chemistry — and why we co-opt it for classification
11. Capstone: the LIGO classifier, end-to-end
12. Where to go next — a tour of the recent literature
