A toy holographic quantum error correction model: a small unitary evaporation circuit that tracks the entanglement entropy of a black-hole subsystem across the Page time. The Page curve emerges when the entanglement wedge of the radiation surpasses that of the remaining hole.

## What you can do

- Adjust system size and evaporation rate
- Watch the entropy curve cross over at the Page time
- Toggle between Hayden–Preskill and Almheiri-style boundary models
