A complete radio-astronomy starter kit calibrated against the 4-meter L-band interferometer at Tardigrade Quantum Laboratory.

**Includes**

- SAWbird H1 LNA with bandpass filter (1420 MHz)
- Airspy Mini SDR with USB cable
- 4-meter yagi antenna construction plans (open hardware)
- Python notebooks for whitening, integration, and Galactic latitude scans
- Calibration data from the Newport rig for sanity-checking your own measurements

**Not included**

- The 4-meter yagi itself (you build it from the plans)
- Mounting hardware (varies by site)
- A computer to run the SDR
