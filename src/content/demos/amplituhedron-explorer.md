Interactive volume sampling over the amplituhedron — the positive Grassmannian region whose volume encodes scattering amplitudes in N=4 super Yang-Mills theory. The demo lets you adjust loop order and external particle count and watch the volume estimate converge.

## What you can do

- Sample at different loop orders
- Project onto lower-dimensional slices
- Compare against known analytic results

The connection to lattice QFT surrogate work is the reason this lives next to Demo 04 — the same Monte Carlo machinery is doing both jobs.
