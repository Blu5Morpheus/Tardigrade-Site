A nitrogen-vacancy magnetometer dev-kit calibrated against the MPCVD reactor build at Tardigrade Quantum Laboratory. Pre-order while the first batch is being machined.

**Includes**

- Optical bench layout (CAD + STL mounts)
- Drive electronics BOM and Gerbers
- Microwave source schematic (can be substituted for any 2.87 GHz source)
- ODMR reduction firmware for STM32
- Calibration walkthrough notebook

**Not included**

- The CVD diamond sample (the magnetometer-grade NV-rich substrate — sourced separately, ~$200–$600 depending on density)
- The 532 nm pump laser
- The objective and CMOS sensor
